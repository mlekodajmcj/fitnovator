At FITNOVATOR, we don’t believe in shortcuts. Hard work, commitment and dedication is what pays off in the long run. That is why we no longer follow the trends of the fitness industry. We know better. Drawing on over five years of professional experience, research and successful results, we have found the sensible path to sustained health and fitness, and it starts with fasting, shifting to a ketogenic diet and working out in short, satisfying training sessions. No myths, no supplements and no calorie-restricted diets. If you are ready to lose weight, find strength and live your authentic life – free of shame, guilt and societal pressure – it is time to get in touch with the team at FITNOVATOR.

Based in London, UK and serving clients all over the world, FITNOVATOR empowers individuals to find their fitness sweet spot, safely and enjoyably. Founder Olaf Mlekodaj is a Personal Trainer and Nutrition Specialist who has helped hundreds of individuals access optimal health to live their best lives. Whether you are new to working out or a diehard fitness geek, our system is customizable and designed to meet your unique needs.

Our mission

Rejecting dogmatic laws of nutrition, FITNOVATOR believes that understanding how your body really works is essential to become vigorous and joyful.

By developing an innovative weight loss strategy and uniquely sustainable approach to long-term health, we empower people to improve their wellbeing and thrive throughout lifetime.
